# Support – BMH Messaging

We're here to help. Find answers to common questions or get in touch with our support team.

---

## Frequently Asked Questions

**How do I create an account?**
Download the BMH app, enter your Bahrain phone number, and verify it with the OTP code sent via SMS. Your account will be created automatically.

---

**I didn't receive my OTP code. What should I do?**
Check that your phone number is correct and has SMS service. Wait a few minutes and tap "Resend code". If the issue persists, contact our support team.

---

**Are my messages private?**
Yes. All messages are end-to-end encrypted using the Matrix protocol. Only you and your recipients can read your messages.

---

**How do I delete my account?**
Go to Settings → Delete Account. You'll need to verify your phone number with an OTP. Once confirmed, your account and all data will be permanently deleted.

---

**I'm not receiving push notifications. What should I do?**
Make sure notifications are enabled in your device settings for BMH. Go to Settings → Notifications → BMH and ensure notifications are allowed.

---

**How do I start a group chat?**
Tap the + button on the Chats screen, select "New Group", add participants from your contacts, and start chatting.
