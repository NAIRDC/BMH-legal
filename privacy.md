# Privacy Policy – BMH Messaging

**Last updated: June 3, 2026**

BMH Messaging is operated by Nasser Center for Innovation & Consultancy W.L.L. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile application.

---

## 1. Information We Collect

We collect the following information to provide our services:

- **Phone number** — Used for account registration and identity verification via OTP.
- **Messages and media** — Messages you send and receive are stored on our servers to enable communication. All messages are end-to-end encrypted using the Matrix protocol.
- **Device information** — Device tokens for delivering push notifications.
- **Usage data** — Basic usage analytics to improve the app experience.

---

## 2. How We Use Your Information

- To verify your identity and create your account
- To enable messaging between users
- To deliver push notifications
- To maintain and improve our services
- To comply with legal obligations

---

## 3. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored on secure servers located in Ireland (AWS eu-west-1). All messages are end-to-end encrypted using the Matrix protocol, meaning only you and your recipients can read your messages. We use industry-standard security measures to protect your data.

---

## 4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data to third parties. We may share data with:

- **Service providers** — Twilio (SMS delivery), Apple (push notifications), Google (push notifications) — only as necessary to provide our services.
- **Legal authorities** — When required by Bahraini law or court order.

---

## 5. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. When you delete your account, your personal data and message history are permanently deleted from our servers.

---

## 6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

- Access your personal data
- Delete your account and all associated data
- Withdraw consent at any time by deleting your account

---

## 7. Children's Privacy

BMH Messaging is not intended for users under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

---

## 8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes through the app or via SMS.
